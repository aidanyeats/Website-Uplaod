Bleeding Star

Bleeding Star is a collection of nine songs that were written following a warehouse accident I survived last May.

“When I’m at lunch,

sat in the sun,

I loathe my part.

Wishing on bleeding stars,

trying to pay off my car.”

Torn, shot down from the sky; under swollen light. I don’t wanna say, I can take the weight.
